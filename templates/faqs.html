Answers

What are your delivery hours?

Everyday, we deliver from 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Where do you deliver?

We currently deliver within Lagos only, with new locations being added soon. If you're outside of our service area, still share your interest — it'll help us pick our next location.

How much does delivery cost?

Our pricing is simple. Delivery fees are based on the distance between pickup and dropoff locations, and the size and nature of the item.

How do I pay?

We accept direct bank transfers. You may also pay cash on delivery or via our POS when the rider arrives. If you make more than 50 deliveries in a week, we can offer you a weekly payment plan.

Can I schedule delivery in advance?

Yes! You can schedule for up to 3 days ahead

Who makes the deliveries?

Sendhut's fleet of drivers will deliver your items Our driver couriers are highly experienced, yet undergo a thorough training process prior to handling orders.

 Do I have to tip?

This is completely up to you. Our riders make the magic happen, so tips are appreciated but not required. Riders receive 100% of all tips.
