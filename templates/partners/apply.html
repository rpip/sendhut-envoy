Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join Sendhut??

Equipments

  • A 200cc or above motorbike
  • A safety helmet
  • A smartphone – iPhone or Android
Documenatations
  • Valid rider's card
  • Valid motorcycle registration papers. Road worthiness

How will I be paid?

Partners can withdraw total weekly earnings on Saturday. The money can be delivered to either the driver, owner or another number provided during training.

How much do I pay Sendhut?

Sign up is free and there's no time commitments. Sendhut takes only 15% of each delivery.

Can I bring multiple vehicles to the platform?

Yes! There is no limit to the number of vehicles you can bring to the platform.

Who brings the Driver on board?

The owner of the vehicle is responsible for sourcing the driver(s).

Who covers fuel costs and Driver stipend?

The owner covers the fuel costs, driver stipend, and any other expenses that are driver or vehicle related.
